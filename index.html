About me

My name is Stevanie Ribka Silalahi, born in Jakarta on April 2, 2010. I am currently a 11th-grade student at Methodist-2 High School, the firstborn out of two siblings. I enjoy drawing, reading, and cosplaying, quite often picks up new hobbies to fill up free time. I dislikes two-faced people as well as frightening/grotesque imagery. I'm quite playful and cheerful, and usually enjoys teasing close friends. Although I can be a bit awkward around new people, I'm generally easygoing and friendly.

I previously studied at Methodist-8 Elementary School and SMP Negeri 11 Junior High School. Since entering high school, I have developed a strong interest in chemistry and currently participates in an extracurricular program called Bimbel Kimia. I also have experience creating commissioned digital artworks as a side job, Completed over 100 commissioned art pieces for online clients. Im pretty ambitious and i strive to do my best in everything i pursues. I aspire to become an architect and hopes to bring future success to my family. Though not without flaws, i continues to improve myself by turning weaknesses into strengths.
